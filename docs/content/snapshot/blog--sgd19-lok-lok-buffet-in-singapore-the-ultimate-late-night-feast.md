- $19 Lok Lok Buffet in Singapore: The Ultimate Late-Night Feast!
- If you love skewers, dipping sauces, and endless servings of crispy, juicy bites, then a $19 Lok Lok buffet in Singapore is exactly what you need! Lok Lok, a beloved street food originally from Malaysia, has taken the Lion City by storm, offering an affordable and indulgent dining experience. Whether you’re a seasoned foodie or a first-timer, here’s everything you need to know about where to get the best Lok Lok buffet for just $19 per person!
- What is Lok Lok?
- Lok Lok (loosely translated as “boil boil” in Cantonese) is a popular street food concept where skewered ingredients—ranging from meats to vegetables—are cooked on the spot, either deep-fried or boiled, and then paired with a variety of flavorful sauces. It’s the perfect communal meal, best enjoyed with friends and family over good conversation and unlimited skewers!
- Where to Find the Best $19 Lok Lok Buffet in Singapore
- While Lok Lok can often be found at night markets or street stalls, several buffet spots in Singapore now offer all-you-can-eat Lok Lok for an unbeatable price of just $19 per person! Here are some of the most popular places:
- 1. LokLok 1991 by Daniel & Meng
- 📍 Location: Multiple outlets across Singapore
- 🕒 Opening Hours: 6 PM – 2 AM
- 🔥 Highlights: Famous for its crispy, well-marinated skewers and signature homemade chili sauce. Enjoy everything from cheesy hotdogs to succulent seafood at an affordable price.
- 2. Lok Lok Paradise
- 📍 Location: Geylang
- 🕒 Opening Hours: 5 PM – 3 AM
- 🔥 Highlights: Their satay-marinated meats and buttery corn skewers are a must-try. Plus, they offer free-flow drinks to complement your meal.
- 3. Steam & Grill Lok Lok
- 📍 Location: Chinatown
- 🕒 Opening Hours: 6 PM – 12 AM
- 🔥 Highlights: If you can’t decide between grilled or steamed, why not have both? This buffet lets you enjoy two cooking methods in one place.
- What’s on the Menu?
- For just $19 per person, you can expect a variety of delicious skewers, including: ✅ Crispy Chicken Wings
- ✅ Juicy Pork Belly
- ✅ Fresh Prawns & Squid
- ✅ Mushrooms & Assorted Vegetables
- ✅ Cheesy Fish Balls
- ✅ Fried Wantons
- ✅ Quail Eggs & Rice Cakes
- ✅ And so much more!
- Many of these places also offer free-flow sauces—from classic satay peanut sauce to garlic chili and even mala dips for those who love a spicy kick!
- Why You Should Try It
- ✔ Affordable & Unlimited – Where else can you eat as much as you want for just $19? ✔ Perfect for Late-Night Suppers – Most Lok Lok buffets operate until past midnight, making it an ideal choice for supper lovers. ✔ Customizable to Your Taste – Whether you prefer grilled, boiled, or deep-fried, Lok Lok lets you enjoy your skewers exactly the way you like them. ✔ Fun Group Dining – Lok Lok is best enjoyed with friends, making it a great bonding experience over good food.
- Tips for the Best Lok Lok Experience
- 👉 Go with an empty stomach! You’ll want to make the most of the buffet. 👉 Try different cooking styles. Fried Lok Lok is crispy, but grilled or boiled versions let you appreciate the natural flavors of the ingredients. 👉 Experiment with sauces. Some places even let you mix and match different dips to create your own perfect combination. 👉 Pace yourself. Don’t fill up on just one type of skewer—mix it up for the ultimate Lok Lok feast!
